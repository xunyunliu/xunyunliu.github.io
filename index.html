Biography

I completed my PhD study at the Cloud Computing and Distributed Systems (CLOUDS) Laboratory, the University of Melbourne, where my research was supervised by Professor Rajkumar Buyya, Assoc. Prof. Benjamin Rubinstein and Dr. Rodrigo N. Calheiros. My interests mainly reside in robust resource management and task scheduling, aiming to improve the performance and reliability aspects of distributed stream processing systems. I have been involved in various research areas such as application profiling, performance modeling, SLA-aware application deployment, and light-weight fault tolerance mechanisms.
